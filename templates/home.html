How it works

Three steps to your
perfect room

No learning curve. No tutorials needed. Just pick a room and start placing furniture.

1
Choose your room type
Select from 13 room types — bedroom, living room, kitchen, office, restaurant, cafe, gym and more. Each comes pre-loaded with the right furniture options.
2
Place & arrange furniture
Drag furniture from the panel into your room. Rotate, resize and reposition items freely. The 3D view updates in real time as you work.
3
Walk through & export
Switch to walk-through mode to experience your design first-hand. When you're happy, save your design or export it as an image.
